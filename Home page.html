Introduction to us

We are students of the University of Macau. We have established a research group called CISC3003, It focuses on web development. CISC3003 is a computer science course aimed at teaching and exploring theories And the practice of modern web development. As a member of the Web development research group, we are passionate about designing and building all types of web pages. We have conducted in-depth research on key technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which contribute to the creation of An enjoyable interactive user experience. Our group works closely together, with each member bringing their own unique strengths to bear. We are passionate about network development and believe that through teamwork and continuous learning, we will become excellent networks Developers and create unforgettable online experiences for our users. We can't wait to invest our skills and creativity into real projects Bring surprises and inspiration to people through online works.
